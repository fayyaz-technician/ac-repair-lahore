Warning Signs

6 Signs Your AC Needs Immediate Repair in Lahore

Lahore's intense summer heat and dusty environment put immense strain on air conditioners. Ignoring these early warnings can lead to expensive breakdowns or complete system failure. Here is what to watch for.

Weak or No Airflow

A clogged filter, blocked condenser, or failing blower motor is common in Lahore's dusty climate. This restricts cooling and forces your system to work harder.

Water Leaking Indoors

Water dripping from your indoor unit indicates a blocked drain pipe or frozen evaporator coil. In Lahore's humidity, this can quickly damage walls and encourage mold.

Unusual Noises

Grinding, squealing, or banging sounds often point to loose fan blades, worn bearings, or compressor issues. These problems worsen rapidly without professional attention.

Burning Smell

A burning odor is a serious red flag for electrical shorts, overheated wiring, or motor failure. Turn off the unit immediately and call us right away.

Frequent On Off Cycling

Short cycling, where the AC turns on and off every few minutes, strains the compressor and thermostat. This leads to higher bills and a shorter lifespan for your unit.

Sudden Spike in Bills

An unexplained jump in your electricity bill usually means low refrigerant gas, dirty condenser coils, or inefficient internal components. We diagnose the true root cause.

Expert Advice

Keep Your AC Running Efficiently in Lahore

Lahore's weather is tough on air conditioners. Follow these expert tips to maximize cooling, lower bills, and extend the life of your unit.

Clean or Replace Filters Monthly

Dust accumulation in Lahore is heavy. Clean filters improve airflow and reduce strain on the compressor, saving up to 15% on electricity.

Set Thermostat to 24 to 26 Degrees

Every degree lower increases energy consumption by nearly 6%. A moderate setting keeps you comfortable without overworking the system.

Schedule Bi Annual Professional Servicing

Get a complete check up before summer (March) and after the peak season (September) to ensure optimal performance and catch issues early.

Seal Windows and Doors

Leaky seals let cool air escape. This forces your AC to work longer and harder, increasing wear and tear on all components.

Clear the Outdoor Unit Area

Ensure the condenser unit outside has at least 2 feet of clear space. Debris and overgrown plants block airflow and reduce cooling capacity.

Use Curtains and Blinds

Block direct sunlight during peak hours. This reduces the heat load on your AC and helps it maintain the desired temperature faster.

Is it better to repair or replace an old AC?

If your AC is over 10 to 12 years old and requires frequent major repairs like compressor or PCB changes, replacement is often more economical. For units under 7 years old, repair is usually the smarter and more affordable choice.

How often should ACs be serviced in Lahore?
